Student Travel Support

The SocInfo 2012 organizing committee invites applications for small grants to help cover the travel expenses and registration. The award grant has kindly been made available by the previous SocInfo organizers.

The grant awards will be given based on merits and preference will be given, in this order, to:

  1. Student who is the author of a paper (full or short) and will be presenting at SocInfo 2012.
  2. Student who is the author of a poster or demo and will be presenting at SocInfo 2012.
  3. Student who has genuine needs for support.

Applications from students who have not registered to SocInfo 2012 will not be considered.

Interested students should email us the following information by October 31, 2012:
  • paper title and author information (if any)
  • breakdown of estimated travel expenses and the corresponding support from the affiliated institution
  • scanned copy of the student ID (with photo)
  • letter/email of support from Ph.D. advisor
The outcome of student travel support applications will be made known by November 10, 2012.
