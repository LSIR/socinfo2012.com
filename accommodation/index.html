Accommodation

Some selected hotels in Lausanne have retained rooms for the Socinfo 2012 participants. All the rooms must be booked directly by the participants themselves, sending an email to the chosen hotel with:

  • the name of the conference (e.g., use in the email subject "Socinfo 2012");
  • the credit card number;
  • the expiry date on the card.
After the indicated deadlines, the room allotments will be released.

All hotels in Lausanne, upon request, will provide a free pass (valid for the duration of your stay) to use the public transportation network of Lausanne. You can use this pass on the Metro M1 which connects downtown to EPFL. Do not forget to ask for it!
